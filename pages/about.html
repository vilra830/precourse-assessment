About Us

The Pink Coffee Corner is a place where you can relax, have some quiet time while indulging yourself to one of the world's top quality pastries and coffees.

The Pink Coffee Corner started as a small coffee cart situated at one of the busy streets in Melbourne. The name of the store was coined by the owner's daughter who loves coffee and the color pink at the same time. The coffee cart usually opens around 6am to 10am Mondays through Saturdays. Not a day goes by that the pastries and cakes are not sold out. Everyone just loved the food we are selling. One day a regular customer who happened to be in real estates opened up an opportunity for the business to expand and have its own permanent location. In 2022, The Pink Coffee Corner officially opened their first pink cafe in Melbourne.
